COME TO THE BEST PLACE FOR MEXICAN FOOD!!

When people in Belleville want an expertly cooked authentic Mexican dish, they always come to Margaritas Mexican Restaurant & Cantina. We’re family owned and operated and have been in business since 2006. We’re also a trusted member of the Belleville Chamber of Commerce. Visit one of our three locations today, and we’ll show you why people love our food.

We’re open seven days a week for lunch or dinner and offer a relaxing atmosphere for family dinner. We’re also great for a quick bite before heading out for the night. We accept all payment options, can be reached by phone or email, and are open until at least 9 p.m. every day.
